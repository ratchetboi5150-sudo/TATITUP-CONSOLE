Offset X: Moves the console left/right.
Offset Y: Moves the console forward/backward.
Offset Z: Moves the console up/down.
Rotation X (Pitch): Rotates the console up/down.
Rotation Y (Roll): Tilts the console left/right.
Rotation Z (Yaw): Spins the console clockwise/counter-clockwise.
Reset Defaults: Resets all position and rotation offsets back to their default values
